About Me

I am a librarian at the NC State University Library of Veterinary Medicine and a candidate for the Master's Degree in Library and Information Science from UNC Greensboro.

Professionally, I am interested in

  • User Experience
  • Medical Librarianship

I have lived in the Raleigh area with my wife, our daughter, and four cats since 2009, having previously lived in Vermont, New Hampshire, and several locations in my native Massachusetts. I am an amateur painter and I occasionally host trivia nights across the Triangle.
